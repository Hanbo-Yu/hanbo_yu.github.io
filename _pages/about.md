I am a Ph.D. student in cybersecurity at the School of Information Studies, McGill University, supervised by Prof. Steven H. H. Ding. I received my Bachelor’s degree from Queen’s University, Kingston. During 2020–2021, I worked as a Software Engineering Intern at IBM Canada.

Research Interest
------

- Security of cyber-physical systems, software supply chain security, and system-level attack detection.
- System and software security, including execution tracing, and intrusion detection for embedded and distributed environments.
- AI-driven cybersecurity, exploring data-driven vulnerability analysis and intelligent detection for complex systems.
